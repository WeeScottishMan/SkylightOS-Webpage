Highlights

🎮 Gaming-first

Skylight OS is tuned for play first, with a CachyOS-optimized kernel, the scx_rustland scheduler, ananicy rules, and cachyos-settings built into the experience.

  • Optimized for gaming performance.
  • Bundled with Steam and Heroic.
  • Includes auto-updating Proton-CachyOS.

⚡ Bleeding Edge

It uses NixOS unstable together with Chaotic Nyx repositories, giving Skylight OS a fast-moving package base for people who like living a little closer to the edge.

  • Latest packages with rollbacks.
  • Stays stable and reliable.
  • Access to a huge app repo.

🧹 Simple

Skylight OS keeps a minimal baseline for gaming and productivity, avoiding the clutter and unnecessary extras that slow down the overall experience.

  • Minimal setup focused on the basics.
  • GNOME-based default apps for simplicity.
  • No over-the-top theming, just a clean design.

🖥️ Lightweight WM

It uses Niri, an easy, configurable, and minimal scrolling window manager that keeps the interface light while still being comfortable for day-to-day use.

  • Scrolling window management for multitasking.
  • Fast, snappy navigation of programs.
  • Simple, easy-to-learn configuration.

🎨 Customizable

Skylight OS lets you skip the painful setup stage and move straight into shaping the system into something personal, polished, and fully yours.

  • Lots of options for customization.
  • Built for users who want a solid base to customize on.
  • Plenty of themes and icons in the NixOS repos.
